Short Bio

I am now a Ph.D. student at Northwestern University, supervised by Ulas Bagci. Before that, I got my B.Eng degree in computer science from ShanghaiTech University in 2022, working with Diggang Shen.

My research interests include medical image analysis (detection, dose prediction) and machine learning & optimization.
